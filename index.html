I am a first-year Ph.D. student working with Prof. Mark Mueller at HiPeRLab at UC Berkeley. I received my M.S. in Aeronautics and Astronautics at Purdue University, West Lafayette.

During my time at Purdue, I was fortunate enough to work with Prof. Shirley Dyke and Prof. David Cappelleri at NASA RETH Institute.

Before I joined Purdue University, in 2020, I received my M.S. in Space and Engineering Systems at Skoltech and I was supervised by Prof. Dzmitry Tsetserukou in the Intelligent Space Robotics Lab. I obtained my B.S. in Systems Engineering from ADA University in 2018. I was a visiting student in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at METU in 2016 and in the Department of Control Systems and Robotics at ITMO University in 2017.

My research interests include MAVs, UAV design, distributed and collaborative multi-agent systems, networked systems, and planning & control.
